One of the most common issues is finding a reliable USB-C dock that works seamlessly with your Surface Pro. This blog post will guide you through the best USB-C docks for Surface Pro users running Linux in 2026, helping you find the perfect solution for your daily tasks.

    1. Logitech Dock Dock+ (https://amzn.to/3O6F7ZG)#

    The Logitech Dock Dock+ is a top choice for Surface Pro users running Linux. It offers a comprehensive set of ports, including two USB 3.0 ports, two USB-C ports, a Gigabit Ethernet port, a 3.5mm headphone/microphone combo, and a microSD card reader. The dock is also equipped with a 12V power output, which can be useful for charging your Surface Pro or other devices.

    To get the most out of the Logitech Dock Dock+, you’ll need to install the Logitech software. This can be done by running the following commands in your terminal:

    sudo apt update
sudo apt install logitech-dock

    After installation, you can connect your Surface Pro to the dock and start using its features. The software allows you to easily manage your ports and even turn off ports when they’re not in use to save power.
