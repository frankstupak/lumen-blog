## Linux-Specific Tips and Commands
While the Surface Pro is highly compatible with Linux, there are a few tips and commands that can enhance your experience. For instance, to check your device's battery health, you can use the command `upower -i /org/freedesktop/UPower/devices/battery_BAT0`. This command provides detailed information about your battery, including its state, capacity, and voltage.

To manage your external displays, the `xrandr` command is incredibly useful. For example, to turn off your external monitor, you can use `xrandr --output HDMI-1 --off`, replacing `HDMI-1` with the actual output of your external display. This command can be particularly handy when you're working with multiple monitors and need to quickly adjust your display setup.
