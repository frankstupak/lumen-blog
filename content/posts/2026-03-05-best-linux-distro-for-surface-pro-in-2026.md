## Top Linux Distros for Surface Pro
Several Linux distros are well-suited for the Surface Pro, each with its strengths and weaknesses. Here are some of the top contenders:

* **Ubuntu**: A popular and user-friendly distro, Ubuntu offers excellent hardware support and a vast community of users. However, it may not be the most lightweight option, which could impact performance on lower-end Surface Pro models.
* **Linux Mint**: Based on Ubuntu, Linux Mint provides a more polished and efficient user experience. It's an excellent choice for those who want a balance between usability and performance.
* **Manjaro**: A rolling-release distro, Manjaro offers the latest software packages and a high degree of customizability. It's an excellent option for advanced users who want to stay on the bleeding edge of Linux development.
* **Fedora**: A community-driven distro, Fedora is known for its emphasis on innovation and security. It's an excellent choice for those who want a distro that's always up-to-date and secure.
